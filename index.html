Core Workflow

From raw FASTQ sequencing reads to validated insights.

Our containerized, reproducible bioinformatics workflows ensure that raw sequencing datasets are quality controlled, mapped, and mathematically modeled with full scientific rigor.

01
Ingestion & Quality Inspection
Adapter removal, fastp trimming, and automated multi-sample quality control reporting via MultiQC.
02
Alignment & Expression Counting
Ultra-fast genomic mapping using STARSpliced Transcripts Alignment to a Reference - high speed RNA-seq aligner., HISAT2, or de novo transcripts quantification using Salmon.
03
Signal Modeling & Statistics
Differential expression modeling (DESeq2), cell clustering (Seurat/Scanpy), or variant detection (GATK4).
04
Reporting & Visual Dashboards
Clean, publication-ready figures, annotated tables, interactive browser dashboards, and Nextflow pipeline logs.
