Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

RASA Life Science Informatics ("RASA", "we", "us", or "our") operates from Pune, Maharashtra, India. We are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of the personal and scientific data you share with us. This Privacy Policy details our practices concerning data collection, processing, security, and compliance.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to deliver bioinformatics services, QSAR evaluations, molecular modeling, and genomics consultation. This includes:

  • Contact Details: Name, professional email address, organization name, phone number.
  • Project Scientific Data: FASTA sequence files, raw genomic FASTQ sequencing data, drug structures, chemical formulas, and metadata required for bio-informatics analyses.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, cookies, and page navigation history.

3. Data Security & Storage

All scientific files and genomic raw data are stored on secure cloud servers with strict access controls and end-to-end encryption. R&D project data is kept separate and is deleted upon project completion and client sign-off, or retained in accordance with the service level agreement (SLA).

4. GDPR & Compliance

For clients located in the European Union, we act as a Data Processor under GDPR guidelines. We process personal and biological data solely under the explicit directions of our clients (the Data Controllers).

RASA Life Science Informatics is a leading bioinformatics company and CRO in India providing AI-powered NGS data analysis services, RNA-seq analysis, genomics consulting, transcriptomics, epigenomics, metagenomics, and precision medicine bioinformatics solutions for pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and academic research organizations globally.
